Safe final phrasing
A safe and precise final answer is:
The finite-sample profile step is the KKT system of an empirical L2 (PT ) projection problem. The EL ratio itself is obtained from a separate sample-simplex
KKT problem. Under uniform convergence, Gram stability, sieve approximation
control, EL convex-hull conditions, and uniqueness of the population identifying equation, these finite-sample equations converge to the population L2 (P )
KKT/moment condition, whose unique solution is β0 . Hence the identified parameter is recovered.

Unsafe phrasing to avoid
Avoid the following statements:
 “EL is an L2 (PT ) KKT problem.”
 “Finite-sample KKT alone proves consistency.”
 “Uniqueness is automatic.”
 “The L2 (PT ) projection converges to L2 (P ) without uniform regularity conditions.”
