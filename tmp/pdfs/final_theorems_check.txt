Remark 1 (Why the segmentation completion is high-level). The martingale restriction
E(Ut | Ft−1 ) = 0 does not imply E(Ut | DT ) = 0, because DT contains future regressors
and those regressors may reveal Ut . Also, a finite (2 + δ)-moment does not by itself yield a
log T search bound for a quadratic form. Thus the Bai–Perron module is not free from the
primitive martingale assumptions. One must either verify Assumption 12 or cite an adapted
Bai–Perron theorem that supplies the same growing-K profile-sieve drift, fluctuation, and
rank conclusions.

local-alternative calculation.
    At the null value β0 , define

9    End-to-End Assumption Audit
This section records the full proof audit for the Bai–Perron replacement workbook. It sep-
arates algebraic definitions from the assumptions that must be proved or imported. In
particular, the Moore–Penrose inverse makes the projection matrix defined, but it does not
rescue asymptotic regularity when a candidate segment is too short, when the basis is locally
collinear, or when the profile criterion lacks a usable drift.
